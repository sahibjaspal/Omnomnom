Purpose: This experiment is aimed for displaying different background colors for different widths. This will also display different colors in the backgrounds of different mobile and tablet devices.





For example:

1. The background color will be blue for a browser with a width less than 500px. The background color will be blue if the site is viewed on a phone.

2. A medium width browser of above 500px will have a background color of green. The background color be green if the site is viewed on a tablet.

3. A browser with width above 700px will have a background color of red.
